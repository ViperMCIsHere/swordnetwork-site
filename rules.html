SwordNetwork Rules

Respect SwordNetwork

We are always trying to improve. Anyone can help us improve. If you don't want to help us that's fine, but do not complain. The title says it all. This also applies when talking to other members and staff.

Advertising

Sharing another server's name or any link counts as advertising. Exceptions may be made for large servers such as Hypixel or MinemenClub. You are only allowed to share YouTube links inside clips-pics-and-highlights and support channels.

Channels

Only use channels for their specific purpose.

Unfair Advantage

The use of any client modification that gives an advantage over vanilla Minecraft is not allowed. Abusing glitches in the game or client is not allowed. Teaming with players who are not on your team is not allowed. Knowingly teaming with players using forbidden modifications is also not allowed. Lag machines are not allowed.

Account Sharing

Sharing, selling, or giving your SwordNetwork account to someone else is not allowed. If you share your account you are responsible for anything that happens including losing ranks, bans, mutes, or other punishments.

Chat Rules

No spamming or flooding the chat except in spam channels. Swearing is allowed unless used in a derogatory or harmful way. Do not discuss NSFW topics. Do not tag people for no reason. Do not threaten or heavily grief other players. Racism, sexism, or discrimination of any kind is not tolerated. Always be respectful and friendly.

Why We Have These Rules

SwordNetwork is intended to be a fun place to play and relax without problems. We want a safe and friendly environment for everyone.

By playing on SwordNetwork or using any of our services you automatically agree to these rules.
