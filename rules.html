Server Rules

Follow these rules to keep SwordNetwork fair and enjoyable for everyone.

Respect SwordNetwork

We are always trying to improve. Anyone can help us improve. If you don't want to help that's fine, but do not complain or attack staff or players. Always remain respectful.

No Advertising

Sharing another server's name or links counts as advertising. Exceptions may exist for very large servers like Hypixel. Advertising without permission may result in punishment.

Use Channels Properly

Use Discord and in-game channels for their intended purpose. Spamming or misusing channels may lead to mutes.

No Unfair Advantage

Any modification that gives advantage over vanilla gameplay is not allowed. This includes cheat clients, exploits, or lag machines.

No Account Sharing

Sharing, selling, or transferring accounts is not allowed. You are responsible for all actions performed on your account.

Chat Conduct

No racism, harassment, discrimination, or threats. Swearing is allowed but abusive behavior is not. Keep chat friendly.
