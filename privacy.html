Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 04/03/2025

Operator

SwordNetwork is operated by A.S (alias: Opulince) as an independent hobby project located in Kuwait.

Contact Email: vipermcishere@gmail.com

Information We Collect

When you connect to SwordNetwork, the following technical and gameplay data may be automatically collected:

  • Minecraft UUID
  • Player username
  • IP address used for connection
  • Login timestamps
  • Logout timestamps
  • Playtime statistics
  • Chat messages and chat logs
  • Command usage logs
  • Gameplay statistics and leaderboard data
  • Punishment records (warnings, mutes, bans)

Purpose of Data Collection

Collected data is used strictly for the operation and improvement of the server. This includes:

  • Maintaining server stability and security
  • Preventing cheating or exploitation
  • Enforcing server rules
  • Investigating player reports
  • Operating gameplay systems such as leaderboards and statistics
  • Improving server balance and performance

Data Retention

  • Chat logs and command logs are typically retained for approximately 45 days.
  • Technical connection logs may be stored temporarily for server security.
  • Punishment records may be stored indefinitely to enforce rule violations.
  • Gameplay statistics may persist until seasonal resets occur.

Third-Party Services

SwordNetwork relies on third-party services to operate the server infrastructure.

  • FalixNodes — server hosting infrastructure
  • Discord — community communication platform
  • Tebex — future store platform for optional server purchases

These services may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

SwordNetwork is intended for users aged 13 years or older.

  • Users under the age of 13 may only access the server with verified parental consent.
  • Accounts suspected to belong to users under 13 without consent may be suspended until consent is provided.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and administrative measures are taken to protect stored data.

However, no internet service can guarantee complete security. By using the server, you acknowledge that data transmission over the internet carries inherent risks.

User Rights

Players may request the following by contacting the operator:

  • Access to stored personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of certain stored information

Requests must be submitted through the contact email. Final decisions regarding data access or deletion remain with the operator.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in server operation, services, or legal requirements.

Major updates may be announced through official server channels or Discord.

Continued use of the server after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
