Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Information We Collect

When you play on SwordNetwork we may collect basic information necessary to operate the server including:

  • Minecraft username
  • IP address
  • Gameplay statistics
  • Connection data

How We Use Data

The data collected is used for:

  • Operating and maintaining the Minecraft server
  • Preventing cheating and abuse
  • Moderation and enforcement of rules
  • Improving server features and performance

Data Sharing

SwordNetwork does not sell or distribute player data. Data may only be shared with trusted service providers required to operate the server such as hosting services.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect player data. However no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Player Rights

Players may request information about their stored data. Requests may require proof of account ownership.

Data Retention

Some data such as punishments or gameplay statistics may be retained for moderation and operational purposes.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when necessary. Continued use of the server means you accept any updates.
